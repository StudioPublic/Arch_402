Community Focussed Adaptive Reuse

“According to the EPA, it takes about 65 years for an energy-efficient new building to save the amount of energy lost in demolishing an existing building. “

Adaptive reuse offers a sustainable path forward by reducing the amount material and energy needed to provide new programmatic use cases in the built environment, while also linking to a historical context and character that would otherwise be discarded and forgotten. Some of the most innovative architecture of the 21st century is adaptive reuse.

Students will be asked to explore adaptive reuse concepts focussed on elevating a select community in Los Angeles through the injection of a unique and forward thinking programmatic strategy and architectural form.

Projects will be developed through conventional 2d and 3d strategies, but we will be investigating experimental strategies utilizing the latest tools in the generative AI landscape during the ideation phase of design.
